Vikings sailed to North America.

The Vikings used the runes to write.

The Vikings did not wear horned helmets.

The Vikings only had two meals in a day: Dagmal (Day Meal) and Nassmal (Night Meal)

The Vikings were very skilled at building boats. They were not at all afraid of the sea.

The Norse people lived in simple, rectangular houses. The houses were often made of timber from the area in which they lived.
